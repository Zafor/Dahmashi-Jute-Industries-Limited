Jute is a natural fiber and called “Golden Fiber” for its luster and color like gold. It is understood that jute was being cultivated on the soil of Bangladesh from very ancient time.

Jute grows well in the tropical countries having much rainfall and hot climate. Bangladesh is the real home for growing the best quality and much quantity of jute. Other countries that grow jute are India, Srilanka, Brazil and Indonesia. In Bangladesh it grows mostly in greater Faridpur, Mymensingh, Comilla, Pabna, Rangpur, Magura and Rajshahi.

Dahmashi Jute Industries ltd is a 100% export oriented jute yarn manufacturing company Established in 2017. We went to production from March, 2018. As its sister Concern Company Golden Jute Industries Ltd established in 2012 and Pride Jute Mills Ltd established in 2003 so the company already has experience in this sector. The factory is located in the district of Faridpur which is known as the best quality of jute growing area of Bangladesh. We are now capable of doing 18000 MT (approx) production per annum. In Golden Jute Industries we do 10000 MT and in Pride Jute Mills Ltd we do 5500 MT. So in total our production capacity is 33500 MT.

Our Major export goes to Turkey, China, Belgium, Russia, Poland and India.

Our Managing Director Moniruzzaman Monir is one of the Director Of the Bangladesh Jute Spinners Association.
